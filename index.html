How do I care for my plants?
We understand that keeping your plants healthy is essential. To help you become a green thumb, we provide detailed care instructions for each plant on its product page. Additionally, our blog features valuable tips and articles on plant care, watering schedules, and more.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and Discovery. You can also use PayPal for a secure and convenient payment experience.

As of now, we only offer shipping within South Africa. We hope to expand our reach internationally in the future, so stay tuned!

What is your return policy?
We take pride in the quality of our plants and ensure they are in perfect condition when they leave our nursery. If your plant arrives damaged or doesn't meet your expectations, please contact our customer support within 7 days of delivery. We'll be happy to assist you with returns or exchanges.

How do you ensure the plants' safety during shipping?
To guarantee that your plants arrive safe and sound, we employ robust packaging methods that secure each plant in place and protect them from any damage during transit.
